Know before you go

Logistics

Base — all 3 nights

Lake Yellowstone Hotel & Cabins. Historic, lakeside, central. No WiFi in rooms — that's the point.

Book by phone (it's peak weekend)

The online form is request-only. Call for live availability:

307-344-7311

Backup if Lake is full

Gardiner sits on your Day 4 exit route — The Roosevelt Hotel ran ~$316/night recently. West Yellowstone is the other gateway.

Wheels

Rental car from BZN is essential — no in-park transit. Fill the tank whenever you pass a station; they're sparse inside.
