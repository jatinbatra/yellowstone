★ Bucket-list

Grand Prismatic — overlook trail

The boardwalk barely sees the color. The Fairy Falls overlook (~1.2 mi RT) is where the photo actually exists. Late afternoon for fewer crowds and best light.

Day 2 · Midway~45 min
★ Bucket-list

Hayden Valley at sunrise

Best bison + grizzly density in the park, 20 min from your hotel. Be in the valley by 5:45 AM. Pull-offs only — wildlife always has right of way.

Day 3 · dawn20 min from Lake
★ Bucket-list

Artist Point + Lower Falls

The yellow-cliff canyon shot. Walk a section of the South Rim trail for the angle most people skip. Lower Falls is twice the height of Niagara.

Day 3 · morning~1.5 hr
Geyser

Old Faithful · time it right

Eruptions every ~90 min, predicted ±10. Check the board at the visitor center the moment you arrive, then plan everything else around the next window.

Day 2~2 min eruption
Geyser

Upper Geyser Basin walk

Don't just see Old Faithful and leave. Walk the 2-mile loop past Castle, Grand, Riverside — more geysers in one square mile than anywhere on Earth.

Day 21.5 hr
Wildlife

Lamar Valley (drive-through)

"America's Serengeti" — wolves, grizzlies, herds of bison. Detour from Tower on Day 3 if you have light left. Adds ~1.5 hr round trip from the road north.

Day 3 · afternoonOptional
Photo

Mammoth Hot Springs terraces

Travertine staircases that look like a frozen waterfall. Upper Terrace loop is a short drive. Hit on Day 1 inbound or Day 3 outbound — both pass through.

Days 1 + 345 min
Geyser

Norris Geyser Basin

The hottest, most dynamic basin in the park. Porcelain Basin loop is otherworldly. Pass through on Day 1 inbound.

Day 11 hr
Easy win

West Thumb Geyser Basin

Five-minute detour right next to Lake. Hot springs that bubble straight into Yellowstone Lake. Most people skip it. You shouldn't.

Know before you go

Logistics

Park entry

$35 vehicle pass covers 7 days in both parks (same ticket if you hit Tetons same day). If you'll see ≥3 NPs this year, $80 America the Beautiful annual pays for itself.

Live road & closure alerts

Check the morning of — bear jams, ice, fires, construction can shut entire loops.

↗ NPS road status

Cell + WiFi reality

Verizon works at Mammoth, Old Faithful, Canyon, Lake village. Nothing at Hayden, Lamar, most of Tetons. Hotel rooms have no WiFi — that's why this page works offline.

Fuel

Gas at Fishing Bridge (next to Lake), Old Faithful, Canyon, Mammoth, Tower. Fill up whenever you pass one — distances between stations are real.

Bear spray

Buy or rent in West Yellowstone / Gardiner / inside the park — TSA won't fly it. Carry on every walk off pavement, including short ones.

Wildlife distance

25 yards from bison/elk, 100 yards from bears/wolves. People get gored every season. Bring binoculars instead of getting closer.
