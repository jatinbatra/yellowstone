Upper Geyser Basin walk

Don't see Old Faithful and leave. Walk the 2 mile loop past Castle, Grand, Riverside. More geysers in one square mile than anywhere on Earth.

Know before you go

Logistics

Park entry

$35 vehicle pass covers 7 days in both parks (same ticket if you hit Tetons same day). If you'll see ≥3 NPs this year, $80 America the Beautiful annual pays for itself.

Live road & closure alerts

Check the morning of. Bear jams, ice, fires, and construction can shut entire loops.

↗ NPS road status

Cell + WiFi reality

Verizon works at Mammoth, Old Faithful, Canyon, and Lake. Nothing at Hayden or Lamar. Save offline maps before the trip and install this page.

Fuel

Gas at Fishing Bridge (Lake), Old Faithful, Canyon, Mammoth, and Tower. Fill up whenever you pass one. Distances between stations are real.

Bear spray

Buy or rent in Gardiner before you enter the park. TSA won't fly it. Carry on every walk off pavement, including short ones.

Wildlife distance

25 yards from bison/elk, 100 yards from bears/wolves. People get gored every season. Bring binoculars instead of getting closer.
